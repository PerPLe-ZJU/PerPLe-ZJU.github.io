About me

Dr. Qi Ye is a Tenure-Track Professor at Zhejiang University. She was a research scientist in the Mixed Reality & AI Lab at Cambridge, Microsoft. She obtained her Ph.D. degree from Imperial College, a Master's degree from Tsinghua University, and a Bachelor's degree from Beijing Normal University. Her research interest lies in the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics, and robotics, particularly in 3D vision and emboided AI. My current research direction includs 3D reconstruction of scenes and hand-object under interaction, active vision, multiview multimodal perception, dexterous robotic manipulation etc. These works have been published in top-tier conferences and journals in computer vision and robotics TPAMI, ECCV, CVPR, ICCV, RAL, ICRA, IROS, etc.

Meanwhile, I am looking for Master students, PhD students, Postdoc and Research Intern, aiming at innovation in computer graphics, computer vision, and robotics.
